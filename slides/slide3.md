<b> WhatsApp Community Strike Management System </b>\
\
As an admin in a large WhatsApp community, I noticed that keeping track \
of members who had broken community rules was difficult and inefficient \
as other admins and I would often struggle to remember how many strikes \
an offender had.\
\
This sometimes led to issues where members would claim they were banned \
unfairly due to admins misremembering the number of strikes a person had \
and banning them too early, or disputes over the number of strikes a member \
had.\
\
To solve this problem, I decided to develop a web-based strike management system \
that would allow regular members to view their own and others strikes, as well as \
allow admins to create, update, and delete strike entries from a centralised database.
